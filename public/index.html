ABOUT


Minesweeper is a single player game where you have to open all cells of the game grid, without opening mines. Each cell shows the number of mines it is surrounded by on the eight sides. The game has three modes: easy, medium and hard which differ in the size of the grid and number of mines. To know more, check the How To Play section. Click on the Play Now button to start playing, complete in minimum time to get featured on the leaderboard!


HOW TO PLAY


Minesweeper involves a grid of cells. Initially all cells are balnk. You can click on a cell to reveal its contents. A cell can either be a mine or it will contain a number which denotes the number of mines which surrond this cell on the 8 sides. In case you click on a mine, you loose the game. You can add a flag on the cells you suspect are mines by right clicking on the cell. There the same number of flags available as the number of mines in the grid. When all the mines are correctly flagged and all other cells opened, you win the game. The time taken to solve a grid becomes your score, which is used to determine your rank on the leaderboard.
